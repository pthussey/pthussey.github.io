Hello, my name is Paul Hussey. Welcome to my blog!

About me

  • I'm orginally from Canada but have been living in Korea since 2002.
  • I work in talent acquisition, connecting people from around the world to exciting roles in Korea.
  • I have advanced working-level proficiency in Korean.
  • I started to learn Python at the tender age of 41 and found that I love programming.
  • I've also dedicated a lot of time and effort to learning how to do data / statistical analysis using Python and found that I love data analytics too.
  • I'm building my programming and data skills so I can apply them to the work I am doing now, and will continue to do in the future, in recruiting and the broader talent management space.

About this blog

I chose to create this blog from scratch using the Flask framework, so I could learn a bit about web development in the process of setting it up. You can read about how I built it in this post.

In my posts on this blog I intend to write mainly about two topics: talent management and programming.

When I write about talent management, I plan to share my views on concepts I encounter through my work and reading in this space. My work is primarily in talent acquisition so I have a fair amount to say on recruiting best practices, but I also think a lot about what motivates people to stay in or leave their jobs after they are hired. As such, I plan to get into topics like corporate culture, leadership, and D&I as well in my posts.

When I write about programming, my posts will document how I went about learning new skills. There are plenty of people out there with far more expertise than me that have produced excellent tutorials on how to use different programming tools, so I don't plan on trying to recreate any of that. Instead I hope to provide more of a learner's point of view with my posts by describing my learning process, providing links to the resources I used, and sharing the problems I encountered and how I eventually solved them.

I hope what I share here on this blog will be insightful and helpful to others who have interests similar to mine.

If you would like to connect with me I'm active on LinkedIn, and if you would like to check out my coding projects, including the one for this blog, please visit my Github.
